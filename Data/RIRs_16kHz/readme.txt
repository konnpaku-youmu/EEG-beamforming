HMA_rirs_back.mat -- RIRs for the two _back_
microphones of the head-mounted microphone array.

HMA_rirs_front.mat -- RIRs for the two _front_
microphones of the head-mounted microphone array.

LMA_rirs.mat -- RIRs for the 5 microphones of
the linear microphone array.

Each .mat file contains two elements:
- data [structure]: RIRs + all necessary info (as produced by GUI)
- thetaSorted [array]: corresponding (ordered) angles, in degrees.
